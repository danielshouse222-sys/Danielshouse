Shipping & Orders

How fast does shipping arrive?+
Standard orders ship within 1-2 business days from our North American fulfillment center. Domestic delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. Free shipping on all orders over $75.
Do you ship internationally?+
Yes — we ship to most countries. International orders typically take 7-14 business days. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and order weight.
How can I track my order?+
You'll receive a tracking link via email as soon as your order ships. Click the link anytime to see exactly where your package is.
Can I change or cancel my order?+
Yes — if your order hasn't shipped yet. Email us within 2 hours of placing your order at daniel@danielshousewellness.com and we'll catch it before it leaves the warehouse.

Returns & Guarantee

What's your return policy?+
Every order is backed by a 30-day satisfaction guarantee, counted from the day your order arrives. If you're not happy with what you bought — for any reason — return it for a full refund. Opened or unopened, partial bottle or empty, doesn't matter. We'd rather you give an honest product a real try and not love it than be locked into a routine that doesn't fit. The policy applies to single products, bundles, and subscription shipments equally.
How do I start a return?+
Email returns@danielshousewellness.com with your order number, or use the returns portal in your account. We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label within one business day — return shipping is on us, never the customer. Drop the package at any carrier location; once we receive it, refunds process to your original payment method within 5 business days.
Can I return a bundle — even just part of it?+
Yes — full or partial. Return the entire bundle for a complete refund of what you paid. Or return individual products from a bundle and get a prorated refund based on the bundle's per-unit price (not retail). Example: the AM Routine costs $155 for 6 products, so each is effectively $25.83. Return one for $25.83 back, two for $51.66, and so on. This way the bundle math stays fair: you keep the bundle discount on what you choose to keep, and don't get retail credit for items that came in at a bundled rate. The 30-day window starts when the bundle arrived.
How do returns work for Build Your Own routines?+
Build Your Own routines follow the same 30-day window and prorated rule, with one nuance: your discount tier is locked at purchase. If you ordered Mix 9 at 15% off and return 4 products, you keep your 15% discount on the 5 products you kept — we don't drop you back down to Mix 6's 12% retroactively. The refund is calculated on the prorated per-unit price you actually paid. Example: 9 products for $255 = $28.33/each. Return 4, get $113.32 back, keep the remaining 5 at $28.33/each. Same applies for partial returns on Mix 3 and Mix 6 tiers — your locked-in tier stays locked.
What about subscription orders?+
The same 30-day window applies to every shipment — not just your first order. Don't love this month's box? Return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund of what you paid. Cancel, pause, or skip your next shipment anytime from your account — no fees, no minimum commitment, no friction. The next shipment only ships if you're still actively subscribed at the renewal date.
What if I've already used most of the product?+
Still eligible within 30 days. Supplements need a few weeks to show effect; skincare needs at least a full cycle of use to know if it works. You can't know if a product fits without actually trying it. Send back what's left — partial bottles, nearly-empty bottles, doesn't matter to us. If a third of a 30-serving bottle and you want a refund, you get a refund. The point of the guarantee is to let you commit fully without the fear that you're locking in something that won't work.
What about damaged shipments, wrong items, or skin reactions?+
Damaged or wrong items: email support@danielshousewellness.com with a photo. We'll ship the correct/replacement item immediately at no charge — and you don't need to return what arrived broken. Adverse reactions: stop using the product right away, then email us with details. We'll refund in full even outside the 30-day window if a product caused an adverse reaction. Reactions are rare with our formulations, but when they happen they're never the customer's fault — and we don't make people pay for a product that hurt them.

Subscribe & Save

How does Subscribe & Save work?+
Pick any product or bundle and choose the Monthly delivery option — we'll ship it every 30 days. Every subscribe option gets an extra 10% off. On a single product, that's 10% off the regular price. On a routine or bundle, that 10% stacks on top of the routine/bundle discount — so an AM Routine at 15% off becomes 23.5% off when subscribed. You can change frequency, swap products, skip a delivery, or cancel anytime — no calls, no friction. Manage everything from your account.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes — completely. Cancel from your account dashboard with one click. No phone calls, no retention pitches, no penalty. The discount is our thank-you for the commitment, not a trap.
Can I change shipping frequency?+
Yes — the default is 30 days, but you can set 45 or 60 day intervals if a bottle lasts you longer. Update from your account anytime.
Do routines and bundles work with Subscribe & Save?+
Yes — every routine and bundle on the site can be subscribed to, and every subscription gets an extra 10% off on top of the routine or bundle discount. This includes the 9 curated routines (Daniel's Daily, AM, PM, Workout, Longevity, Glow, Mom's, Ariana's, Ultimate), the 15 targeted bundles, and the tiered Build Your Own routine you create from the picker (10% at Mix 3, 12% at Mix 6, 15% at Mix 9+). Example: a $182 AM Routine is $155 one-time (15% off), or $139.50 subscribed (15% routine + 10% subscribe = 23.5% total). Cancel or pause anytime.
How does the Build Your Own routine discount work?+
Pick more, save more — Build Your Own is tiered: Mix 3 saves 10%, Mix 6 saves 12%, and Mix 9 or more saves 15%. The discount applies to whatever combination of skincare and supplements you pick. Subscribe to stack an extra 10% on top of any tier. Example: pick 9 products totaling $300 → $255 one-time (15% off), or $229.50 subscribed (15% + 10% = 23.5% total). Curated routines (AM, PM, Mom's, Ariana's, etc.) all sit at 15% — so once you cross the 9-product line, you're at the same discount whether you pick yourself or use one of Daniel's routines.
Can I subscribe to a Build Your Own routine?+
Yes. Build your custom routine in the picker (any tier — 3, 6, or 9+ products), choose Monthly delivery, and we'll ship that exact combination every 30 days. You get your tier discount (10%, 12%, or 15%) plus an additional 10% off for subscribing — they stack. Want to swap something next month? Edit your subscription from your account — no need to cancel and rebuild.
What is Smart Refill?+
Smart Refill is the smarter way to subscribe to a mixed routine. A standard monthly subscription ships every product every month — but real products don't all run out at the same pace. A 3-month NAD+ bottle on a 30-day cadence means you stockpile two unused bottles a year. Smart Refill solves this: each product in your routine ships only when you actually run out. A 1-month Multi ships monthly, a 2-month Wash ships every 60 days, a 3-month NAD+ every 90 days. Same routine, no stockpiling, no waste — and on the Ultimate Routine alone, Smart Refill saves roughly $2,168 per year versus monthly delivery of everything.
How is Smart Refill different from monthly Subscribe & Save?+
Both keep you subscribed; both stack the 10% subscriber discount on top of the routine/bundle discount. The difference is cadence per-product. Monthly sends every product on the same 30-day cycle — simple, predictable, but you over-receive products that last longer than a month. Smart Refill ships each product on its actual usage cycle (30 / 60 / 90 days depending on bottle size) so you only pay for what you'll actually use that month. Lower average monthly bill, less clutter on your shelf, same total products over the year. Choose whichever fits your style — both are available on every routine and most bundles, both can be paused or cancelled anytime from your account.
Which routines work with Smart Refill?+
Most routines and concern bundles support Smart Refill — including the AM Routine, PM Routine, Glow Routine, Longevity Routine, Mom's Routine, Ariana's Routine, the Ultimate Routine, and any Build Your Own routine you create. A few of the tightly-packed routines (Daniel's Daily, the Foundation supplement stack, the Workout stack, the Weekly Reset) ship all products on the same cadence by design and don't offer Smart Refill — for those, Monthly Subscribe is the right option. The product page will show whichever cadence options apply to that routine.

Ingredients & Safety

Where are products manufactured?+
All Daniel's House products are manufactured in FDA-registered, GMP-certified facilities in North America. Every batch is third-party tested for purity, potency, and contamination before it leaves the warehouse.
Are products vegan?+
Most are vegan — our skincare line is 100% vegan and cruelty-free. In supplements, exceptions include Flow (fish-based omega-3) and Collagen (bovine collagen). Each product page lists this clearly.
Can I use products if I'm pregnant or nursing?+
For skincare, avoid retinol-containing products (Bounce, Renewal). Pregnancy-safe alternatives include Hydration, Soft, Hyaluronic, Defense, and Eye. For supplements, consult your healthcare provider before starting any new supplement during pregnancy or nursing.
Are products gluten-free?+
Yes — all Daniel's House products are gluten-free. Common allergen warnings (soy, dairy, tree nuts, fish) are listed on each product page where applicable.
Can I take supplements with prescription medication?+
For specific medications, consult your doctor or pharmacist. Supplements that warrant extra caution: Tranquil (ashwagandha) with thyroid medication, Flow (omega-3) with blood thinners, Restore (turmeric) with certain anti-inflammatory drugs. Most stacks are well-tolerated, but your healthcare provider knows your situation best.
How do I store products?+
Skincare: cool, dry place out of direct sunlight. Most products last 6-12 months after opening. Supplements: cool, dry place. No refrigeration required even for our probiotic (Biome uses MAKTREK technology for shelf stability).

Using the Products

In what order should I apply skincare?+
Three rules cover almost everything: thinnest to thickest, water before oil, actives at night, antioxidants in the morning.

AM: Wash → Balance (toner) → Boost (Vit C) → Hyaluronic (on damp skin) → Defense → Soft (moisturizer). End with SPF if you use one.

PM: Wash → Balance → Renewal (resurfacing) or Bounce (combo treatment) — pick one, alternate nights → Firm (peptides) → Hydration → Eye → Glow (face oil, final seal). Add Mask or Polish 1–2x/week, replacing your treatment that night.

Never stack retinol and AHA the same night — your barrier will tell you why.
How long until I see results?+
It depends on the product. Sleep and energy supplements often shift within 1-2 weeks. Skincare changes are typically visible at 4-6 weeks for tone/texture, 8-12 weeks for fine line reduction. Longevity supplements compound over months and years. Consistency beats intensity — daily use is the variable that matters most.
Can I combine multiple products?+
Yes — most are designed to be combined. Our routines and bundles are pre-built combinations Daniel actually uses. The main rules: don't double up on retinol products in skincare (Bounce + Renewal alternate, never together); follow caffeine cutoff times for stim-containing supplements like Burn.
Do I need to take supplements with food?+
Fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, K) absorb best with meals containing some fat. This includes Multi, Sunshine, Flow, and Vitality. Calm and Synapse can be taken with or without food. Each product page specifies the optimal timing.
What if a product doesn't work for me?+
Send it back within 30 days for a full refund. Wellness is individual — what works for thousands may not be right for you. We'd rather you find a product that does work than feel stuck with one that doesn't.
