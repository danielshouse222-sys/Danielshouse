Give $15, Get $15.

The strongest brands grow because someone you trust says "you should try this." If Daniel's House has been worth that recommendation for you, we want to make it worth your friend's time too.

Give $15. Share your unique referral link with a friend. They get $15 off their first order over $50.

Get $15. When your friend places their first order, you get a $15 credit toward your next order.

How It Works.

  1. Get your link. Log into your account and grab your unique referral link from the Refer a Friend page.
  2. Share it. Text, email, Instagram, however you talk to your friends. We have ready-made messages if you want a starting point.
  3. They order. Your friend uses your link to shop. Their $15 off is applied automatically at checkout (on any order over $50).
  4. You earn. Once their order ships, your $15 credit lands in your account. Use it on your next order — no expiration.

The Details.

  • Your friend must be a new customer (no prior Daniel's House orders)
  • Their order must total $50 or more after any discounts
  • You get $15 in store credit, not cash — usable on any future order
  • Credit doesn't expire — stack as many as you want
  • Referral credits can stack with subscription discounts and promo codes
  • No limit on how many friends you can refer

Why We Built This.

We're a small brand by design. We don't have a $50M ad budget and we don't want one — that's exactly the markup we're not charging on the formulas. The growth model that makes Daniel's House work is people recommending us to people who'll actually use what we make.

The $15-each-way isn't a gimmick. It's literally what one customer-acquisition cost looks like in a healthy brand — and we'd rather pay it to you than to Meta.

Common Questions.

Can I refer myself?

No — referrals are for new customers other than yourself. Multiple accounts to game the system will get both accounts suspended.

What if my friend already has an account?

Your link won't apply for them — referrals are first-order only. If they've never ordered but signed up for the newsletter, they're still eligible.

Can I use my $15 credit and a promo code on the same order?

Yes — referral credits stack with most promo codes and subscription discounts. Some promotional codes (like "free shipping" or "double credit days") may have stacking restrictions noted at use.

Do referral credits expire?

No. Stack as many as you want, use them whenever.

What if my friend returns their order?

If a referred order is returned in full within 30 days, the corresponding $15 credit is reversed from your account. Partial returns don't affect your credit.
