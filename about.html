The Story

Why this house exists.

For ten years I bought wellness products that made promises and didn't keep them. Hundreds of dollars on supplements that didn't move the needle. Skincare that was either over-engineered with twelve actives or stripped down to nothing useful. The category was full of marketing and short on results.

In 2023 I started building my own routine from scratch. I read the studies myself. I ordered ingredients tested at proper doses, in forms my body could actually absorb. I cut what didn't work and kept what did. The results — sleep, energy, skin, focus — showed up over the first six months and have compounded ever since.

Friends and family started asking what I was using. Then asking me to help them build their own routines. Then asking why I didn't just sell what I'd put together. So Daniel's House started.

"This is the wellness brand I would have wanted to find when I started. Premium ingredients, properly dosed, paired together intelligently — not picked off a shelf because the bottle looked good."

Every product in the house is something I personally take or use. Every ingredient is at a clinically-meaningful dose, in the form your body can absorb. Every routine is the routine I actually follow. The skincare is the skincare I put on my face every morning. The supplements are the supplements that sit on my counter.

The brand is named Daniel's House because that's what it is: my house, the products I use, opened up to the people who want what I built. No celebrity endorsements. No influencer-led launches. No marketing spin on ingredients that don't earn their place.

Just real results — the kind that compound when you commit to a routine that actually works. Welcome to the house.

The Principles

What every product earns.

01.
Clinical doses, not marketing doses.
Every active is at the dose used in the studies that prove it works. We don't sprinkle in trace amounts to put an ingredient on the label — if it's not at a clinically-meaningful dose, it's not in the bottle.
02.
Forms your body can actually use.
Magnesium glycinate, not magnesium oxide. Ascorbyl phosphate, not oxidized Vitamin C. KSM-66 ashwagandha, not generic powder. The form matters as much as the dose — we use what's been validated in clinical research.
03.
Paired to compound, not just shelved.
Vitamin C with E for antioxidant synergy. Curcumin with BioPerine for absorption. Resveratrol with quercetin. Every product is built knowing what it's typically taken with — and the bundles work because of that.
