Our review promise.

Every review here will be from a verified customer. No paid reviews, no incentivized reviews, no review-trading. If a customer received a free product (which we sometimes do for early supporters or content creators), that disclosure will appear next to the review.

We publish the critical reviews next to the positive ones — same algorithm, same visibility, no hiding. If someone gave us two stars and a clear explanation, you'll read it. Our 30-day guarantee means people who didn't like a product got their money back, so they have nothing to gain from leaving an unfair review — but we still publish whatever they wrote.

Daniel reads every review personally and responds to the ones that need a response — when someone's frustrated, when there's a real question, when something can be fixed. That's the small-brand advantage we don't want to lose.
